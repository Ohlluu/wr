Our mission is to engage and unite a network of local residents and organizations through training and capacity-building to leverage our collective power and shape our communities. We work to build a just, livable and vibrant Greater West Side.

About Westside Rising

Westside Rising is a Coalition dedicated to building a Just, Livable & Vibrant Greater West Side. We are boots on the ground 365 days a year, not just talking about change, but actively creating it.

Community Organizing

We engage local residents and organizations through training and capacity-building to leverage collective power and shape communities.

Leadership Development

Our training programs have produced multiple organizational leaders who got their start through Westside Rising's comprehensive development initiatives.

Civic Engagement

Encouraging active participation in local government and democratic processes.

Our Programs & Initiatives

Building leadership, empowering youth, and creating lasting change in Chicago's Greater West Side.

Leadership Training Programs

Comprehensive organizing and leadership development training that has launched the careers of multiple organizational leaders across Chicago's West Side.

Youth Ambassador Program

Helping young people learn about their community and heritage while developing leadership skills and civic engagement capabilities.

Community Resource Events

Regular events including food distribution, back-to-school supplies, free clothing, haircuts, and family activities like bouncy houses and games.

Upcoming Events

Join us for our signature events including Light Up Lawndale, Together WE Rise Holiday Soiree, Juneteenth Celebrations, and Restorative Justice Summits.
